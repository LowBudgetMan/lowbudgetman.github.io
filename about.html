About Me

My journey in software engineering began with a fascination for solving complex problems, enjoying the process like solving a puzzle, and creating useful user experiences, across all types of interfaces. Over the years, I've developed a deep appreciation for clean code, thoughtful architecture, and collaborative development practices. Since finishing college and launching into the industry in 2016, I've been fortunate enough to have many great mentors and be heavily involved with practitioners of the agile mindset. This strong influence is what drives much of the way I write software.

As for the why I write software, I love solving problems and making peoples' lives better. The low barrier to entry helps it feel like something can come from almost nothing with the right magical incantation. I love that so many things can be built to enhance people's lives from the mundane to the meaningful. I don't want to create tech for the sake of making it, I want the things I do to improve lives even if just a tiny amount. I endeavor to do things like digitize workflows because it will improve the quality of the experience of the people using them, not just because I can.

All that is to say, I specialize in quality, extensible code developed fast mainly in the web space. I'm a fullstack engineer with the most experience in React SPAs and Spring Boot REST APIs. I hope you find the things I've built to be useful, and the things I say to be helpful.
